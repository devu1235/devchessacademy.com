Who We Are

Dev Chess Academy is a vibrant hub for chess enthusiasts. We aim to nurture talent, inspire passion, and empower players to achieve their full potential.

Founded by experienced chess coaches, our academy blends modern techniques with timeless strategies, catering to beginners and advanced players alike. Join us to embark on a journey of growth and mastery.

To make chess accessible and enjoyable for everyone, fostering strategic thinking, patience, and creativity through our expert coaching programs.
